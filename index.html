Gap to target
At $15/lead, 37% contacted and 3% conversion, the plan yields 4.3 acquisitions/week — short of the 6/week target. Closing the gap needs 200 contacted leads/week (vs 143 modeled): either lift conversion to 4.2% at today's contact rate, or lift contacted rate to ~52% at 3% conversion. Both levers act on the same driver — more contacted volume per week.
